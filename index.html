About Me

I am 21 years old and from Prosper, Texas. I am currently a Junior at Brigham Young University and preparing to apply to the Marriot School of Business for the Marketing program. I recently returned from a 2 year French and Tahitian-speaking mission in French Polynesia for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I love to travel and am always up for an adventure! I have a lot of interests and various skills developed by persuing those interests throughout my life. Overall, I love creating meaningful relationships with others around me and playing or watching nearly all sports.

French Polynesia

I lived for a year in the beautiful islands of French Polynesia including Tahiti, Raiatea and Huahine. The islands are a beautiful ocean oasis, filled with coconuts, diverse tropical fruits, fishes of every color and size and ocean water in every shade of blue.
